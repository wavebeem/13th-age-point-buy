How to Use

Click the checkboxes and plus and minus buttons in the following table to distribute your ability score points and bonuses.

All stats are for a 1st level character and assume that you picked the highest armor without an attack penalty and did not take a shield.

Computed Modifiers

The following modifiers are added onto your base AC, PD, and MD values which can be found in the page about class stats per level. These modifier does not include the +level modifier for various stats.

AC (middle of CON / DEX / WIS)
PD (middle of STR / CON / DEX)
MD (middle of INT / WIS / CHA)

Racial Bonus

Add +2 to one ability score determined by your race. This bonus is applied after your point buy.

Class Bonus

Add +2 to one ability score determined by your class. This bonus is applied after your point buy and cannot be the same as your racial bonus.
