13th Age Point Buy

Ability Scores Calculator

Instructions

Click the checkbox or the label next to it to select an ability score as favored, giving it a +2 bonus.

Columns are as follows:

  • Ability score before favored bonus
  • Ability score after favored bonus
  • Final ability score modifier

Cost: / 28

Racial Bonus

Add +2 to one ability score determined by your race. This bonus is applied after your point buy.

Class Bonus

Add +2 to one ability score determined by your class. This bonus is applied after your point buy and cannot be the same as your racial bonus.
